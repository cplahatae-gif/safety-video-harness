# Imagegen Scenario Test: BCT Collision Prevention

Date: 2026-06-14

Purpose: Test Codex built-in imagegen with a storyboard-first safety-video flow.

Scope:
- 30-second safety training concept
- 4 keyframes only
- No narration or TTS
- No Seedance or paid video generation
- Korean teaching copy is planned as subtitles/overlays outside generated keyframes

Style:
- Korean industrial webtoon safety style
- Crisp line art, realistic adult worker proportions, controlled cel shading
- Same ready-mix plant, white BCT tanker, yellow dump truck, green pedestrian lane, orange PPE accents

Topic:
- Ready-mix concrete plant collision/contact prevention at a vehicle entry and blind-spot zone

Source basis:
- Existing harness fixture/project topic: remicon collision/contact prevention, BCT, dump truck, signal worker, blind spot, reverse/entry control

## Scenes

### sc01 - Hazard Context

Scene role: hazard_context

Visual beat:
Wide establishing frame at the plant entry. A white BCT tanker approaches a shared vehicle route. A yellow dump truck waits ahead near a blind-spot zone. The green pedestrian lane and black-yellow bollards clearly show separation between vehicle and worker zones.

Visible cast:
- BCT driver visible through windshield
- Signal worker in white hard hat and orange reflective vest

Safety point:
Large vehicle entry routes create blind spots around stopped or turning trucks.

Subtitle/overlay outside image:
차량 진입 전 사각지대와 보행 동선을 확인한다

Continuity:
Sets the same plant, BCT, dump truck, signal worker, and pedestrian lane for the next scenes.

### sc02 - Stop And Confirm

Scene role: safety_control

Visual beat:
The BCT has stopped before the crossing/hazard zone. The signal worker stands in a marked safe position and gives a clear stop signal. The driver looks toward the visible side mirror and the signal worker.

Visible cast:
- Same BCT driver
- Same signal worker

Safety point:
The vehicle must stop before entering the shared zone and confirm the signal.

Subtitle/overlay outside image:
정지 신호를 확인하고 진입을 멈춘다

Continuity:
Begins from sc01 approach and prepares sc03 verification.

### sc03 - Verify Safe Route

Scene role: verification

Visual beat:
The signal worker points toward the safe vehicle path after checking the pedestrian lane. A ground worker remains behind the safety line, clearly outside the vehicle path. The driver keeps eye contact through the mirror/cab direction.

Visible cast:
- Same BCT driver
- Same signal worker
- One ground worker behind the safety line

Safety point:
Signal guidance works only when pedestrians remain outside the vehicle path and the driver verifies the route.

Subtitle/overlay outside image:
보행자는 안전선 밖, 운전자는 신호와 거울을 동시에 확인

Continuity:
Connects stopped confirmation to controlled movement.

### sc04 - Controlled Safe Movement

Scene role: safe_resolution

Visual beat:
The BCT moves slowly through the guided route. The signal worker maintains a visible guiding gesture from the safe zone. The pedestrian lane remains clear, and the dump truck stays parked without conflict.

Visible cast:
- Same BCT driver
- Same signal worker
- Ground worker remains behind the safety line

Safety point:
Only after stop, signal, and route confirmation should the vehicle proceed slowly.

Subtitle/overlay outside image:
확인 후 저속 진입, 보행 동선은 비워둔다

Continuity:
Final safe state suitable as the end keyframe for a short validation video.
